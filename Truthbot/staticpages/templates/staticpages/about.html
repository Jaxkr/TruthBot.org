Truthbot is an online database of news that anyone can contribute to. It offers transparency on the ownership structures of news organizations, as well as a community-powered news aggregator for under-covered topics.

Supporting Truthbot

The best way to support Truthbot is to contribute news articles and add to the organization database! However, Truthbot is a project I work on in my personal time. I pay for the server and other costs all out-of-pocket. I don't mind doing this, but donations are extremely appreciated so I can continue to develop Truthbot and keep it online.

If you want to contribute financially to Truthbot, you can do so via Bountysource.
